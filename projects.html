Parallel GenSel

This is my senior project, so it’s still in progress. The goal of the project is to investigate using CUDA to parallelize GenSel which is a Genetic Selection program written in C++. Our target for parallelization is the BayesC algorithm (specified in this paper) as it’s the currently most used algorithm.

Weightlog (Title in progress)

This is my current personal project. I have over 5 years of logged workouts in a couple notebooks and I wanted a tool to analyze them. My plan for this is a simple interface that allows entering past lifting sessions quickly and easily. At 4 sessions a week (possibly an understimation), 52 weeks a year, 5 years, that’s over 800 workouts. I want it to eliminate a lot what I feel is the redundant parts of logging eg warmups but it should still give room for variation (I don’t always do 10 reps with a weight to warm up, but most of the time I do). The main point of it is to visualize and easily parse data about my workouts such as total volume each workout for a lift plotted with how my lift maxes change at the time. Planning to use node.js and possibly angular.js (although I’m not sold on angular yet).
